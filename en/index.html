Questions and Answers

Do I need to know how to write prompts?
No. You can pick a ready-made example and recreate it with your own appearance. If you know how to write prompts, you can create an image from your own request.
Where does the generation happen?
In the Telegram bot. The website shows what is possible and quickly takes you to Telegram.
Can I create product photos?
Yes. You can create product cards, still-life visuals and images for ads, marketplaces, websites and social media.
Can I restore an old photo?
Yes. You can use AI scenarios to improve old, blurry or damaged photos.
Are my photos published automatically?
No. Results are only published if you choose to. After generation you can save, delete, regenerate or publish the image.
What is the Sandbox?
It is a section where users can submit their own ideas and images. If the community likes a piece, it can be promoted to the public catalog.
Does the creator get bonuses?
Yes. If a user scenario becomes popular and enters the catalog, the creator can receive bonuses for its usage.
Can I create an image from my own prompt?
Yes. There is a custom prompt mode for advanced users.
Can I do an AI photoshoot?
Yes. Pick a ready-made photoshoot style, upload your photo and get an image in the chosen look.
Is the service suitable for business?
Yes. The service can be used for product cards, still-life photography, ad images and social media content.
